---
type: FaultTreeEvent
id: FTE-ENG-001
title: Crankshaft position sensor total failure
eventKind: basic
ref: System::Sensors::CrankshaftPositionSensor
failureRate: 3.0e-7
probability: 3.0e-4
---

Total loss of crankshaft position signal. Causes engine to use estimated
position, which may result in incorrect injection timing and abnormal torque.
Detected by the safety monitor within two engine cycles.
