The engine stalls unexpectedly at high speed, causing sudden loss of drive
torque and potential loss of power steering and brake assistance.

## Hazard description

A stall at high speed can cause:
- Loss of engine braking
- Loss of power-assisted steering (if electrically driven, mitigated)
- Reduction in brake assist if vacuum-based booster is used
- Potential for rear-end collision or secondary incidents
