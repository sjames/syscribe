The engine delivers torque exceeding the driver's demand due to a fault in the
electronic throttle control system. The vehicle accelerates unintentionally.

## Hazard description

Unintended acceleration can arise from:
- Electronic throttle control software fault commanding excessive throttle opening
- Throttle actuator mechanical fault (stuck-open)
- Sensor fault causing incorrect pedal demand reading

At vehicle speed and S3 severity, this presents a risk of collision or loss of
control leading to serious injury or fatality.
