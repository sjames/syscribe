id: REQ-ENG-PERF-000
name: Engine ECU shall meet response time and fuel efficiency targets
status: approved
reqDomain: system
derivedFrom:
  - REQ-ENG-SYS-000
breakdownAdr: ADR-ENG-SYS-001
---

The Engine ECU **shall** respond to driver throttle demand within specified latency
limits and maintain fuel delivery accuracy within specified tolerances under
all steady-state and transient operating conditions.
