title: Closed-loop fuel control shall maintain lambda within 2 % of target
status: approved
reqDomain: software
derivedFrom:
  - REQ-ENG-PERF-000
breakdownAdr: ADR-ENG-PERF-001
verificationMethod: test
---

The closed-loop fuel control **shall** maintain the air-fuel ratio within
±2 % of the stoichiometric lambda target (λ = 1.00) during steady-state
operation above 1000 rpm and coolant temperature above 60 °C, as measured
by the wideband lambda sensor.
