---
type: Requirement
id: REQ-ENG-SAFE-000
title: Engine ECU shall prevent safety hazards identified in HARA
status: approved
reqDomain: system
derivedFrom:
  - REQ-ENG-SYS-000
breakdownAdr: ADR-ENG-SYS-001
---

The Engine ECU **shall** implement safety mechanisms sufficient to prevent or
mitigate the hazardous events identified in the Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment
(`Safety::HARA`), achieving the ASIL targets assigned to each safety goal.
