---
type: Requirement
id: REQ-ENG-SYS-000
title: Engine ECU shall provide safe and efficient engine management
status: approved
reqDomain: system
---

The Engine Control Unit **shall** manage fuel injection, ignition timing, and
electronic throttle control to deliver efficient engine operation while maintaining
functional safety (ISO 26262) and cybersecurity (ISO/SAE 21434) obligations under
all operating conditions defined in the vehicle system specification.
