Level Crossing LC-001 is located on the approach to Station 1 and crosses the road giving access to the station forecourt. The crossing is activated before any train route that includes the approach section is cleared. The vital processor requires barrier-down confirmation from this module as a pre-condition for clearing Home Signal A (down direction). The crossing SIL 3 integrity is consistent with the EN 50159 Category 2 communication to the vital processor and the overall system SIL 4 claim.
