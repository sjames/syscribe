Points Machine 1 controls the facing junction at the Station 1 approach. This points machine determines whether arriving trains are directed to platform 1 (normal position) or platform 2 (reverse position). As a facing points installation (trains pass over it facing the switch blade), it is subject to the full EN 50128 / EN 50129 SIL 4 vital locking and detection requirements. The points must be detected in the correct position before any approaching route is cleared.
