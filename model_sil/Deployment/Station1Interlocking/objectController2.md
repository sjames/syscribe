Object Controller 2 serves the points machines and the level crossing at Station 1. It interfaces the vital processor to the two Points Drive Modules (pointsDrive1 and pointsDrive2) and the Level Crossing Module (levelCrossing1) via the EN 50159 Category 2 field bus. OC2 translates movement commands into drive circuit activations, reads detection contact states, and reports barrier status on every polling cycle.
