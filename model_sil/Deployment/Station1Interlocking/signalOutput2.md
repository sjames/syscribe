typedBy: System::Hardware::SignalOutputModule
domain: hardware
---

Home Signal B controls train movements in the up direction (departing from Station 1 toward the main line). Aspects controlled: red (most restrictive), yellow (caution), green (proceed). This signal is cleared only when the platform is clear, the facing junction points are in the correct position, and no conflicting route is set in the down direction.
