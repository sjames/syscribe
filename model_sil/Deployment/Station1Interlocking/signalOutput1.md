Home Signal A controls train movements in the down direction (arriving at Station 1 from the approach section). Aspects controlled: red (most restrictive), yellow (caution), green (proceed). This signal is cleared only after all route conditions are met including platform vacancy, points position, and level crossing barrier confirmation for LC-001.
