Object Controller 1 serves the home signals at Station 1. It interfaces the vital processor to the two Signal Output Modules (signalOutput1 and signalOutput2) via the EN 50159 Category 2 field bus. OC1 translates aspect commands from the vital processor into relay energisation commands, and reports signal lamp and relay feedback to the vital processor on every polling cycle.
