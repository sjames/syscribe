Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment package. Contains hazardous events and safety goals for the CBI system, derived per IEC 61508-5 Annex D risk graph parameters. All risk classification uses IEC 61508 parameters: consequence (Ca–Cd), frequency of exposure (Fa/Fb), avoidance possibility (Pa/Pb), and demand rate (W1/W2/W3).
