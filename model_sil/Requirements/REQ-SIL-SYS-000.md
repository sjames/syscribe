---
type: Requirement
id: REQ-SIL-SYS-000
title: Railway interlocking system shall prevent unsafe train movements in all operating conditions
status: approved
reqDomain: system
---

The CBI system shall implement railway interlocking logic that prevents physically conflicting or unsafe train movements in all operating conditions, including degraded modes (loss of one vital channel, trackside equipment failures, communication errors). The system shall achieve SIL 4 integrity (IEC 61508 Table 2: dangerous failure rate < 10⁻⁸ /h) for all Safety Instrumented Functions identified in the HARA. EN 50129 safety case required for system approval.
