---
type: SafetyGoal
id: SG-SEC-001
name: Prevent unintended vehicle acceleration
status: approved
asilLevel: D
safeState: Throttle driven to fail-safe position; engine at idle torque
ftti: 100ms
---

The vehicle **shall** prevent unintended acceleration. This safety goal is the
cross-domain anchor: a cyber threat that leads to a safety-tagged damage
scenario can violate it (ISO 26262 ⇄ ISO/SAE 21434 co-analysis).
