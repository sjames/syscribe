# Electric Vehicle DC Fast-Charging Station

A MagicGrid (V2) MBSE model of a public **DC fast-charging station** for electric
vehicles. The model is organised after the Dassault/No Magic *MagicGrid* method:
a **Problem domain** (Black box B-row and White box W-row) and a **Solution
domain** (S-row), each spanning the four pillars **Requirements (1)**,
**Behaviour (2)**, **Structure (3)** and **Parameters (4)**.

The system of interest is a multi-stall charging station that draws power from
the medium-voltage grid, manages energy delivery to connected EVs, authenticates
drivers, bills them, and reports telemetry to a back-office cloud.
