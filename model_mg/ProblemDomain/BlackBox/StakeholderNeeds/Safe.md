id: REQ-EVCS-NEED-003
title: "Drivers and bystanders need the charging process to be electrically safe"
status: approved
requirementKind: stakeholder
reqDomain: system
verificationMethod: test
custom_fields:
  mg_cell: B1
---

High-power DC charging must not expose drivers, vehicles or bystanders to
electrical hazards: the station must isolate energy on fault and only energise
the connector once a valid, safe vehicle connection is confirmed.
