title: "Drivers need a fast charge so a typical EV gains usable range in a short stop"
status: approved
requirementKind: stakeholder
reqDomain: system
verificationMethod: test
custom_fields:
  mg_cell: B1

EV drivers expect a public DC fast-charging station to deliver a large fraction
of usable range during a short stop (comparable to a refuelling stop), so that
long-distance travel is practical.
