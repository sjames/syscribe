The UAV system shall comply with all applicable airworthiness and operational regulations for the EASA Open Category A3 sub-5 kg class, including maximum take-off mass limits.

## Rationale

Operating in regulated airspace requires demonstrated compliance. This stakeholder goal is decomposed into a mass requirement verified by physical weighing.
