Requirements for the build system integration capability: the `buildExports:`,
`buildVar:`, and `buildOverrides:` schema fields, the `build-config` subcommand,
variable resolution rules, and associated validation diagnostics.

All requirements derive from `REQ-TRS-BUILD-000` and are governed by
`ADR-SYS-BUILD-001`.
