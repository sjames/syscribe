The UAV product line shall be offered in survey, mapping, and delivery
configurations built from a common core, with each configuration meeting its
variant-specific performance objectives.

## Rationale

Stakeholder goal for the product line. Decomposed per `ADR-SYS-PLE-001` into one
variant-specific leaf requirement per differentiating capability, each conditioned
on the feature that introduces it.
