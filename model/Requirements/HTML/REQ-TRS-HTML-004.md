`Diagram` elements shall be embedded in the site and shall render without network access.

## Behaviour

- SysML diagram kinds (BDD, IBD, StateMachine, Sequence, Requirement) shall be embedded as
  inline SVG generated by the model renderer.
- Mermaid diagrams shall be embedded as client-side Mermaid blocks, rendered by a Mermaid
  script bundled into the output (no CDN).
