The UAV system shall meet all mission performance objectives, including minimum flight endurance, navigation position accuracy, and command/telemetry data link range required to complete the planned survey mission.

## Rationale

Mission operators require a coherent performance envelope. This stakeholder goal is decomposed into three verifiable system requirements covering endurance, navigation, and communication.
