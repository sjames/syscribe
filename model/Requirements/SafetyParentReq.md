The UAV system shall not cause injury to persons or damage to property during any phase of operation, including nominal flight, contingency events, and loss-of-control scenarios.

## Rationale

Stakeholder-level safety goal derived from regulatory requirements and operator duty of care. All derived safety requirements (sensor fault detection, safe landing, link-loss handling) trace to this goal.

## Notes

This is a stakeholder-level requirement with no `derivedFrom`. Derived requirements: REQ-UAV-SAFE-001, REQ-UAV-FC-001.
