Requirements (`REQ-TRS-BL-000..012`) for release-baseline management: the `Baseline` element
type and schema, the git-anchored full-content seal and per-element manifest, scope
resolution, the `baseline` command family (`create` / `verify` / `diff` / `list` / `show`),
the validator-frozen drift/immutability rules (`E520`/`W520`/`E521`/`E522`), and documentation.

All requirements derive from `REQ-TRS-BL-000` and are governed by `ADR-SYS-BASELINE-001`.
